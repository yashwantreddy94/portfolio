Who We Are

Your Trusted Travel Partner Since 2019

Safareasy is a Guwahati-based travel brand under Ovatree International Private Limited, born with one goal — to make quality travel accessible to everyone. From budget flyers to luxury seekers, we craft experiences that fit your dreams and your budget.

Headquartered in the heart of Assam, we serve travellers across Northeast India and beyond, offering everything from quick flight bookings to full-service holiday packages.
