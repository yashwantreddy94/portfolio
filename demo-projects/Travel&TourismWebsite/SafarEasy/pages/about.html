Our Story

Born to Make Travel Accessible

Safareasy was established in 2019 by Ovatree International Private Limited with a simple but powerful vision — to make quality travel accessible to every income bracket. What began as a focused flight booking solution quickly evolved into a full-spectrum online travel platform.

Headquartered at 152, Rajgarh Main Road, Bhangagarh, Guwahati – 781007, Assam, we are proud to be a homegrown Northeast India travel brand serving travellers across the country and beyond.

Today, Safareasy offers a wide range of travel services including domestic and international flight bookings, hotel reservations, curated holiday packages, visa assistance, cruise bookings, and travel consultancy — all under one roof, with transparent pricing and instant discounts.
